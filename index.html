Our Story

Proudly Filipino.
Crafted with Purpose.

A's Pinoy Foods, LLC was founded to share the authentic flavors of Filipino cuisine with families, restaurants, and retailers across America.

Inspired by the culinary heritage of Pampanga, our mission is to create high-quality Filipino food products that honor tradition while meeting modern expectations for quality, consistency, and food safety.
